MIYH - ABOUT

Maths In Your Head was established in 2004 and has been diligently keeping children, students, and teachers up to date with their mental arithmatic.

The global use of MIYH has increased significantly as the years have progressed, going from 20 monthlyusers in June 2005, all the way to over 3 million users a month now in May 2020.

Teachers worldwide have praised MIYH for the last 12 years as we have progressed and made more efficient and useful resources.

MIYH has been gifted many awards of excellence such as the 2008 global Maths Webapp Awards and the 2013 International Award for Excellence in Maths Tutoring.
